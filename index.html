RESEARCH AREA

研究方向聚焦於生成式人工智慧（Generative AI）與跨模態應用（Multimodal Application），特別關注語言模型在圖像生成任務中的語意理解與邏輯推理能力。致力於探討模型在接收包含數量資訊的文字提示時，如何正確解讀並生成對應數量與樣式一致的圖像物件。
設計的架構結合了多模態大型語言模型（MMLLM）與思考鏈策略（Chain of Thought, CoT），將圖像生成任務拆解為語意理解、背景與物件生成、以及空間組裝等模組化流程，藉此提升語句的邏輯連貫性與生成圖像的數值準確性。此技術不僅能解決主流生成模型在處理「物件數量」時常見的錯誤，也為教育場景提供了新的應用方式。透過視覺化抽象數學概念（如加減法中的加數拆解），ANOVA 能幫助學生更直觀理解數學邏輯，尤其適合早期教育與特殊學習需求者。本研究同時也關注這類生成技術在實際應用中的可行性與穩定性，包括與使用者互動時的準確性與系統設計的可解釋性，期望未來能進一步將 AI 圖像生成技術應用於教育、創意設計、數位助理等多樣場域。

Research lies at the intersection of generative artificial intelligence (GAI) and multimodal application, with a particular interest in how language models interpret and reason about quantity information during image generation tasks.
The architecture, which leverages Multimodal Large Language Models (MMLLMs) and Chain of Thought (CoT) strategies to decompose complex text-to-image processes into distinct modules: prompt interpretation, background and object synthesis, and spatial arrangement. This modular pipeline significantly improves both the logical coherence of model reasoning and the numerical accuracy of generated visuals.Unlike many current T2I systems that struggle with object count fidelity, ANOVA ensures precise generation of user-specified quantities while maintaining consistent visual style. This enables its use in educational settings, where visual clarity and numerical correctness are essential — for example, helping students visualize arithmetic problems using color-coded objects, improving accessibility for young learners and students with special needs.Beyond technical accuracy, the research also explores the broader usability and reliability of such systems in real-world applications — from educational tools to human-computer interaction — with the goal of building explainable, responsive, and context-aware multimodal AI systems.
